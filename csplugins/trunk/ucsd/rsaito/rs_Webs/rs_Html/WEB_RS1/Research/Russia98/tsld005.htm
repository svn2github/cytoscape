Filtering GenBank data

  • GenBank data with keywords gpseudo genesh,himmunoglobulinh, grecepterh, gmitochondriah, gpartialh are discarded.

  • Redundancies of data were reduced by program CLEANUP (Grillo et al. 1996)

  • There may be some data with their ORFs not properly assigned.
