Negative Selection of AUG triplets near start codons in eucaryotes

  • Frequencies of AUG triplets are significantly low in 5fUTR.

  • Frequencies of AUG triplets rise sharply immediately after start codons.

  • Those phenomena are presumably due to translation initiation mechanism.
