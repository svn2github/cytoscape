Conclusions

  • AUG triplets around start codons have been negatively selected.

  • Distributions of AUG triplets are different between procaryotes and eucaryotes, presumably due to different mechanism of translation initiation.

  • Higher organism are more sensitive to spurious AUGs before start codons.
